04/01/26

 - Added soap system, with support for Soaps & Lye soap.
   - Applying soap to yourself while bathing in water will make you soapy, increasing how quickly you get clean.
 - Added perfume system, with support for Soaps & Lye poultices.
   - Applying a soap treated poultice from Soaps & Lye will reduce how stinky you are and hide your smell for a few hours.
 - Bumped default threshold for stink particles to 0.4375 (maxStinkinessDays/4).
